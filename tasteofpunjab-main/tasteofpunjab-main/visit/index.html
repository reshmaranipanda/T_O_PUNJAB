Welcome

Welcome to The Pier Sixty Collection!

Our collection of venues is located on three separate piers within the Chelsea Piers complex:

  • The Lighthouse is at the end of Pier 61, the northern pier at Chelsea Piers, closest to 22nd Street.
  • Pier Sixty is at the end of Pier 60, the central pier at Chelsea Piers, between 19th & 20th Streets.
  • Current is at the base of Pier 59, the southern pier at Chelsea Piers, closest to 18th Street.

Parking

Parking is available on all three piers and lots are located directly in front of each venue. Chelsea Piers hourly parking rates will apply. For rates visit: https://www.chelseapiers.com/also-at-the-piers/parking/

  • For parking at The Lighthouse, take your first right into Pier 61.
  • For parking at Pier Sixty, take your second right into Pier 60.
  • For parking at Current, take your third right into Pier 59.

Taxis & Rideshare

Taxis, Uber & Lyft can drop visitors at the front doors of each venue.

Upon entering the Chelsea Piers complex, instruct your driver to turn right into Pier 61 (The Lighthouse), Pier 60 (Pier Sixty), or Pier 59 (Current) and drive all the way to the back of the parking lot, to the glass front doors at each venue, for drop-off and pick-up. Drivers may remain in the complex for up to 20 minutes without being charged for parking.

Walking

Our event venues are accessible to walkers via crosswalks at 22nd Street (for The Lighthouse), 20th Street (for Pier Sixty), and 18th Street ( for Current). After crossing, please turn off the Chelsea Piers cobblestone roadway and into Pier 59 (Current), Pier 60 (Pier Sixty), or Pier 61 (The Lighthouse), and walk to the back of the pier to find the front doors of our venues.

Citibike

A Citi Bike docking station is located right in the complex on the Chelsea Piers cobblestone roadway between Pier 61 and Pier 60 by 20th Street.

Public Transport

Bus

  • The M23 crosses 23rd Street and drops visitors within the Chelsea Piers complex on the North side.
  • The M14D crosses 14th Street and drops visitors at the South side of Chelsea Piers on 18th Street and the West Side Highway.

 

Subway

Take the following subways to the 23rd street stop and follow walking instructions.

  • #6 – Park Avenue
  • N (Weekends Only), R, W – Broadway/Fifth Avenue
  • F, M, PATH – Sixth Avenue
  • #1 – Seventh Avenue
  • C, E – Eighth Avenue

 

Additional Subway

  • #7 Subway stops at 34th Street & Eleventh Avenue, a 10-15 minute walk south to Chelsea Piers
